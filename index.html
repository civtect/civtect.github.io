About Us

It all started in the year 1988 when Civtect India Private Limited was formed. The first project assigned to us was the construction of the LPG Bottling plant of Indian Oil Corporation Limited at Muchipara and there has been no looking back ever since.

From then onwards, we have forayed into various types of engineering construction. We are not only an engineering firm but also a consulting agency. Because of our technical expertise, we were entrusted with the renovation and re-building of turnkey projects which were built ages before we came into existence.

MISSION: To be the harbinger of a new-age of infrastructure development that stands the test of time.

Over the last three decades, we have become a name to reckon with in the field of engineering and civil construction. A name that has become synonymous with quality, expertise, and diligence.
